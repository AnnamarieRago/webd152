Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and for anyone interested in a grounded foundation to the practice of yoga. This 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), prayanama (breath work) and guided medititation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-sychronized movement.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features slow movement with long poses that are supported by a wall or chair. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be perfect to help rehabilitate an injury.

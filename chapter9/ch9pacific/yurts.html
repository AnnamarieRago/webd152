What is a yurt?

Our Luxury yurts are permanent structures 4 feet off the ground. Each Yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor and a roof dome that can be fully opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen sized bed with a down quilt and a gas fire stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Showers and restrooms are located at the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing.It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!

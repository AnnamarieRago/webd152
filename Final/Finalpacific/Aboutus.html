About Us

Our History Our unique campsite offers a beautiful view of the California Coastline, and has been here since 1915. We opened up with the Panama Canal Exhibition and have stayed open since as a way to represent our California History. We look forward to you joining us in our journey into California's vast expanse of nature and history.

  • In between San Diego and San Fransisco, our campsite is perfect to learn about the history of the Exhibition.
  • Although we were founded in 1915, the family still owns and operates the campsite today!
  • We were built in order to house the influx of people coming to California during the 1915 Era due to the Exhibition, And we still have some original structures standing today!
  • We can give you an opportunity to learn more about our campsite and the Exhibition's history, that is rarely known.
